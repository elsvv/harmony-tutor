# VII43 — Leading-tone seventh chord, second inversion (4-3)

## How to read the label

`VII43` breaks down as:

-   `VII` — the scale degree (leading tone).
-   `43` — the 4–3 figured-bass inversion of a 7th chord (second inversion): the **5th** of the chord is in the bass.

## Structure

Second inversion of the leading-tone 7th chord:

-   **IV** — in the bass
-   **♭VI**
-   **VII**
-   **II**

## Function

Dominant-function chord that often connects smoothly into dominant inversions.

## Practical note

Watch the tendency tones and keep the resolution stepwise where possible.
