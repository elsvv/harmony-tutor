# VII2 — Leading-tone seventh chord, third inversion (2 chord)

## Structure

Third inversion of the leading-tone 7th chord:

-   **♭VI** — in the bass (7th; harmonic-major color)
-   **VII**
-   **II**
-   **IV**

## Function

One of the sharpest dominant sonorities:

-   bass (♭VI) tends strongly to **V**

## Practical note

Make the bass half-step **♭VI → V** clear when moving into the dominant.
