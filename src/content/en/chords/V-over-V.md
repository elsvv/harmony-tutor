# V/V — Dominant of the dominant

## What it is

**V/V** is a dominant 7th chord that resolves to **V7**.

In C major:

-   V/V = **D7**
-   V7 = **G7**

## Where you hear it

-   the Rhythm Changes bridge
-   cadences and dominant chains

## Why it’s useful

It’s one of the most common ways to increase momentum toward the dominant and get a more “jazzy” pull.
