# VII2-harm — Leading-tone 2 chord in harmonic major

## Structure

Third inversion (2 chord) of the leading-tone 7th chord in harmonic major:

-   **♭VI** — in the bass (lowered 6th; 7th of the chord)
-   **VII**
-   **II**
-   **IV**

## Function

Maximum dominant pull, often used right before a clear dominant (D7/D65).

## Practical note

The key motion is the bass half-step: **♭VI → V**.
