# ii7 — Minor seventh chord on scale degree II

## Role in jazz

In a major key, **ii7** is the standard **predominant** chord. It gently prepares the dominant and almost always moves to **V7**.

## Interval content

Minor seventh chord: 1–♭3–5–♭7.

## Typical resolution

Most often:

-   ii7 → V7 → Imaj7

_Tip: keep common tones between ii7 and V7 — that smooth voice leading is a big part of the jazz sound._
